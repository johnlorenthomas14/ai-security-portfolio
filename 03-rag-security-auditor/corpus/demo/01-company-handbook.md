# ACME Corporation Employee Handbook

Welcome to ACME Corporation. This handbook covers our standard operating
procedures, employee benefits, and product documentation.

## Customer Support Hours

ACME customer support is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM
Eastern. Reach us at support@acme.example.com or by phone at our main line.

## Product Documentation

Our flagship product, ACME Cloud Platform, supports multi-region deployment
and enterprise SSO. Full documentation is available on our knowledge base.

## Travel Reimbursement

Submit travel expenses through the standard portal within 30 days of
return. Approved expense categories include lodging, meals up to the
GSA per-diem rate, and standard transportation.
